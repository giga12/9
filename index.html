Description

OxGEn Molecular Solution blood DNA purification kit provides a rapid method for the isolation and purification with a quick and convenient spin column. With OxGEn Blood DNA purification Kit you can high yield of DNA from the blood of mammalian species such as: humans, sheep, dogs and rat. A Sample preparation time is 30 minutes. The purified DNA is of excellent quality and is completely compatible with following applications including PCR, qPCR, SNP, Southern blotting, sequencing and more.
